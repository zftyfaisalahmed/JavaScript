Operators

JavaScript operators are symbols that are used to perform operations on operands.

        1. Arithematic Operators => Addition +, Subtraction -, multiplication *, division /, Modules %, Increment ++, Decrement --

        2. Comparison (Relational) Operators => Is equal to ==, Identical (equal and of same data type) ===, Not equal to !=, Not Identical !==, Greater than >,
        Greater equal to >=, Less than <, less than or equal to <=
        
        3. Bitwise Operators =. Bitwise AND &, Bitwise OR |, Bitwise XOR ^, Bitwise NOT ~, Bitwise Left Shift <<,
        Bitwise Right Shift >>, BitwiseRight Shift with Zero >>> 

        4. Logical Operators => Logical AND &&, Logical OR ||, Logical NOT !

        5. Assignment operators => Assign =, Add and Assign +=, Subtract and Assign -=, Multipy and assign *=, Divide and assign /=, Modulus and assign %=

        6. Special or Ternary operators => condition ? "true":"false"
